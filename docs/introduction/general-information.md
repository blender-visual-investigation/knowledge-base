## Target Audience

This knowledge base serves professionals and students across multiple disciplines:

- **Forensic investigators** - Crime scene reconstruction and evidence visualization
- **Digital forensic analysts** - 3D analysis and spatial documentation
- **3D reconstruction specialists** - Technical modeling for investigative purposes
- **Visual investigation professionals** - Journalists, researchers, and OSINT practitioners
- **Students and researchers** - Academic study of visual investigation methods

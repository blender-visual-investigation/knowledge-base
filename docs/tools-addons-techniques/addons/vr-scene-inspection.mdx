## Introduction

This addon provides a way to view your scene in Virtual Reality directly from the Blender viewport. It uses the OpenXR standard, making it compatible with a wide range of headsets. This allows for immersive inspection of scale and space.

## Installation & Usage

### Installation
This addon is included with Blender.

1. Go to **Edit > Preferences > Add-ons**.
2. Search for **VR Scene Inspection**.
3. Enable the checkbox.

### Usage
Open the Sidebar (N-panel) and find the **VR** tab. Click **Start VR Session** to begin. Ensure your headset is connected and recognized by your OS (e.g., via SteamVR or Oculus app).

# Edge & Vertex Slide

## Edge Slide
Allows you to slide an edge along the adjacent faces.

**Shortcut:** `G, G` (Press G twice)

## Vertex Slide
Allows you to slide a vertex along one of its connected edges.

**Shortcut:** `Shift + V` or `G, G`
